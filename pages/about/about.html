The Insitute is an online group in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas game (GTA:SA) in Multi Theft Auto (MTA) software in CIT Server (Community Integrity Transparency).

The Institute is level 9 Civilian group which i spent many years in that group playing for the wholeday everyday approximately. I liked that family too much and i became famous and one of the important men in group and the server because i was the best in many things in that server before resigning years ago. I really miss this family too much but the life stuffs make it hard to come back.

CIT server is the best server of GTA SA i ever played which created 13 years ago in 2010, they have a very airtight system to handle the community.. in the past there was about thousand active players everyday in this server and the server still alive till now. There are 3 types of groups and squads lives in CIT which are Criminal, Law and Civilian, each community has their real life laws and system like chrief, criminal boss, most wanted, cops vs criminal events, civilian events...etc. Earning ranks in the server forum like being staff or getting any community rank or side rank means that you must met the requirements of this rank, the system is not easy like other games, maybe you need to apply 5-6 times for months to get one rank and be accepted because they won't accept you if you just forget a character.
